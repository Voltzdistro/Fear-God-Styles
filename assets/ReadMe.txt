How to use:
- Place your product and gallery images in assets/images/
  - Update index.html to reference correct file names.

  - Use free icons from https://simpleicons.org/ or https://tabler-icons.io/
- You may replace sample images with your brand images anytime.

Edit WhatsApp phone number:
- Change all `2348088184511` instances in index.html and script.js.
- Update the href for WhatsApp links and the value for whatsappNumber in script.js.

Edit product names/prices:
- Update each product card in index.html with correct title, image, price, and WhatsApp prefill.
